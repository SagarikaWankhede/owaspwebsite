ABOUT

Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology (TIET) has been a steady source of highly skilled talent to the nation as well as overseas. A pioneer in engineering education, research and innovation. The team of OWASP Student Chapter, one of the gilt-edged coding society, involves ingenious mind solvers who are eager to make the world a better place to live in with their innovative techniques and discoveries. By successfully organizing many hackathons, tech-talks, workshops and coding nights, we have always strived hard to maintain the coding culture throughout the campus.

OUR EVENTS

HACKOWASP 6.0

In April 2024, our annual event became a convergence of brilliance and creativity during an electrifying 36-hour hackathon. Guided by the compelling theme “Echoes of the Past, Visions of the Future,” participants dared to merge ancient wisdom with cutting-edge technology. Their groundbreaking ideas not only redefined innovation but also forged a visionary path where the richness of history empowers the technologies of tomorrow.

MYSTICODE

IMystiqode, an overnight event drew a remarkable crowd from every nook and corner of our nation, igniting the intrigue of 2000 attendees eager to delve into the domain of Cyber Security through the theme of Zodiac Killer. Throughout the event, attendees were tasked with deciphering intricate mysteries, each more perplexing than the last

CODIGO

Codigo was a 4-day event conducted in December 2020. It had 4 subevents to test the participants’ skills from deciphering to debugging,to dive deeper into the relm of coding and showing off their marketing skills. With participation of 850+, the event garnered a lot of appreciation.
